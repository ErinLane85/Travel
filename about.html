Years ago I started creating my bucket list, the things I really want to do before I die. Two of my bucket list items: a cross coutry trip to explore the beautiful USA and go to Tennessee to see the synchronous fireflies in the Smoky Mountains (if you don't know about them, check them out here). I'm lucky enough to have some time off and money saved so I decided to take advantage of this opportunity and do something on my bucket list because who knows when I'll have this opportunity again? And if I'm taking advantage of the opportunity, why not squeeze two bucket list items in at one time?
I knew it would be tough but after staying up all night, I was able to score camping reservations at the Elkmont Campground for the first week of June. Now I just had to cross my fingers that the week I picked was a good week to see the fireflies because I wouldn't know the viewing dates for another five months. After much anticipation, I found out the week I reserved for camping is the EXACT week for viewing the synchronous fireflies! I can't believe I got that lucky! It was meant to be!
Planning my route was really tough because I want to see everything! I had to narrow down what I wanted to focus on for this trip and I decided visiting family and Tennessee were my priorities.
Right now, my trip is 6 weeks long, approximately 6,600 miles and covering about 15 states. I plan to post updates along the way with pictures and the route as Penny and I travel. Thanks for coming along on this trip with us!!
